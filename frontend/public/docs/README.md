# UBUNIFU SACCO Ltd.
## Empowering Uganda's Tech Innovators & Creatives

UBUNIFU SACCO Ltd. is a member-owned financial cooperative dedicated 
to empowering Uganda's growing ecosystem of technology startups, 
innovators, and creatives. Established in 2025, UBUNIFU SACCO aims 
to provide affordable financial services, access to startup capital, 
and a vibrant community network that fosters innovation, 
entrepreneurship, and inclusive economic growth.

## Quick Facts

| **Aspect** | **Details** |
|------------|-------------|
| **Established** | 2025 |
| **Location** | Plot 15, Mutungo Hill, Kampala, Uganda |
| **Target Beneficiaries** | Tech startups, innovators, creatives, youth-led enterprises |
| **Project Duration** | 5 years (January 2026 – December 2031) |
| **Total Budget** | UGX 50,000,000,000 |
| **Contact** | info@ubunifusacco.org |

## Our Core Services

- **Savings and Investment Products**: Affordable savings plans, 
fixed deposit accounts, and investment opportunities
- **Startup and Innovation Loans**: Tailored credit facilities for 
early-stage tech startups and creative ventures
- **Business Development Support**: Capacity-building programs, 
mentorship, and advisory services
- **Networking and Collaboration Hubs**: Spaces for members to 
connect, co-create, and access market opportunities
- **Digital and Financial Literacy Training**: Essential skills in 
digital tools, budgeting, business planning, and investment management

## Strategic Objectives

1. Provide reliable financial support systems for startups, creatives, 
and technology entrepreneurs
2. Foster a connected and collaborative innovation ecosystem in Uganda
3. Promote financial literacy and digital inclusion among youth and 
creative communities
4. Build a self-sustaining SACCO model contributing to Uganda's 
development
5. Attract and manage investment capital through shares and strategic 
partnerships

## Why UBUNIFU?

Unlike conventional SACCOs that primarily support agriculture or small 
trade, UBUNIFU SACCO is designed with a sector-specific lens, focusing 
on startups, developers, digital content creators, artists, and 
technologists. This strategic orientation allows for tailored financial 
products such as innovation loans, equipment leasing, and grant 
matching services.
