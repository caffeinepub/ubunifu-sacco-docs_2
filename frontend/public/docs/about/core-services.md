# Core Services

UBUNIFU SACCO offers a comprehensive range of financial and non-financial services tailored to the unique needs of technology startups, innovators, and creatives.

## 1. Savings and Investment Products

| **Product** | **Description** | **Target Members** |
|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **Regular Savings Accounts** | Flexible savings with competitive interest rates | All members |
| **Fixed Deposit Accounts** | Higher returns for longer-term commitments | Members with surplus capital |
| **Youth Savings Schemes** | Tailored accounts for young entrepreneurs | Youth-led enterprises |
| **Investment Shares** | Equity participation in the SACCO | Members seeking ownership |

## 2. Startup and Innovation Loans

| **Loan Type** | **Purpose** | **Features** |
|---------------|-------------|---------------|
| **Seed Capital Loans** | Business startup and prototyping | Flexible terms, mentorship included |
| **Equipment Leasing** | Access to computers, cameras, studio equipment | Asset financing, affordable rates |
| **Working Capital Loans** | Day-to-day operations | Quick disbursement, revenue-based repayment |
| **Innovation Grants** | Competitive grants for high-potential ideas | Non-repayable, milestone-based |

## 3. Business Development Support

- **Mentorship Programs**: One-on-one guidance from industry experts
- **Incubation Support**: Structured programs for early-stage ventures
- **Advisory Services**: Legal, financial, and strategic business advice
- **Market Access**: Connections to customers and distribution channels

## 4. Networking and Collaboration Hubs

- **Physical Hubs**: Co-working spaces in major innovation centers
- **Virtual Platforms**: Online communities for knowledge sharing
- **Industry Events**: Expos, pitch competitions, and networking mixers
- **Partnerships**: Links to accelerators, incubators, and investors

## 5. Digital and Financial Literacy Training

| **Training Program** | **Topics Covered** |
|----------------------|---------------------|
| **Digital Literacy** | Online tools, digital marketing, e-commerce |
| **Financial Management** | Bookkeeping, budgeting, tax compliance |
| **Business Planning** | Business models, pitching, strategy |
| **Investment Readiness** | Fundraising, investor relations, valuation |
