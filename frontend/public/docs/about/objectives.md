# Strategic Objectives

## Objective 1: Reliable Financial Support Systems

> To provide reliable financial support systems for startups, 
creatives, and technology entrepreneurs.

**Key Targets:**
- Establish a UGX 50 billion revolving loan fund
- Develop 5+ tailored financial products for different member segments
- Achieve 90%+ loan repayment rate through responsible lending
- Disburse funds to at least 3,000 tech-driven enterprises within 
5 years

## Objective 2: Collaborative Innovation Ecosystem

> To foster a connected and collaborative innovation ecosystem 
in Uganda.

**Key Targets:**
- Establish partnerships with 15+ innovation hubs, accelerators, 
and incubators
- Create regional chapters in Kampala, Mbarara, Gulu, and Mbale
- Host quarterly tech summits and annual innovation expos
- Facilitate 50+ mentor-matchmaking connections annually

## Objective 3: Financial Literacy and Digital Inclusion

> To promote financial literacy and digital inclusion among youth 
and creative communities.

**Key Targets:**
- Train 10,000+ members in financial and digital skills
- Develop online learning resources accessible via mobile
- Achieve 70%+ digital adoption rate among members
- Conduct outreach in underserved rural areas

## Objective 4: Self-Sustaining SACCO Model

> To build a self-sustaining SACCO model that contributes to 
Uganda's social and economic development.

**Key Targets:**
- Enroll 25,000+ active members by year 5
- Achieve operational self-sufficiency by year 3
- Maintain transparent governance with annual audits
- Generate sustainable revenue from member services

## Objective 5: Investment Capital Mobilization

> To attract and manage investment capital through shares and 
strategic partnerships.

**Key Targets:**
- Mobilize UGX 50 billion through member shares, investors, 
and grants
- Establish partnerships with government innovation funds
- Engage development partners for technical and financial support
- Create diaspora investment schemes for returnees
