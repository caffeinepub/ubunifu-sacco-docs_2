# Company Profile

## About UBUNIFU SACCO Ltd.

**UBUNIFU SACCO Ltd.** is a member-owned financial cooperative dedicated to empowering Uganda's growing ecosystem of technology startups, innovators, and creatives.

## Our Story

Established in 2025, UBUNIFU SACCO emerged from the recognition that Uganda's vibrant tech and creative sectors face persistent barriers to accessing affordable finance and support services. Despite a burgeoning youth population and increasing digital literacy, access to capital remains a critical bottleneck for most entrepreneurs.

The SACCO is uniquely positioned to address the financial and networking needs of youth-led enterprises, tech innovators, digital content creators, and creative professionals by offering tailored financial products and capacity-building programs.

## Our Philosophy

Through collaboration, trust, and a commitment to sustainable development, UBUNIFU SACCO bridges the gap between creative potential and economic opportunity. We believe that when innovators and creatives have access to the right financial tools and networks, they can transform Uganda's economy and society.

## Key Differentiators

- **Sector-Specific Focus**: Dedicated to technology and creative sectors, unlike traditional SACCOs
- **Tailored Financial Products**: Innovation loans, equipment leasing, and grant matching services
- **Integrated Support**: Combining finance with mentorship, networking, and capacity building
- **Digital-First Approach**: Leveraging technology for seamless service delivery
- **Member-Owned Model**: Democratic governance with members as shareholders

## Legal Status

UBUNIFU SACCO operates as a legally registered Savings and Credit Cooperative Organization under the laws of Uganda, in compliance with the Ministry of Trade, Industry and Cooperatives and the Uganda Microfinance Regulatory Authority (UMRA).
