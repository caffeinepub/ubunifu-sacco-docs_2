# Vision & Mission

## Our Vision

> To become the leading financial cooperative in Uganda that supports technology-driven enterprises, empowering creators and innovators to shape a better, inclusive digital economy.

## Our Mission

> To provide accessible, innovative, and inclusive financial services that support startups, creatives, and innovators in Uganda through savings, affordable credit, business development support, and strategic partnerships.

## Core Values

| **Value** | **Description** |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Innovation** | Embracing new ideas and technologies to better serve our members |
| **Inclusion** | Ensuring equal access to financial services for all innovators and creatives |
| **Integrity** | Operating with transparency, honesty, and ethical governance |
| **Collaboration** | Building partnerships that strengthen the innovation ecosystem |
| **Sustainability** | Creating lasting value for members, communities, and Uganda |

## Strategic Focus Areas

1. **Financial Inclusion** – Reaching underserved tech and creative entrepreneurs
2. **Capacity Building** – Equipping members with skills for business success
3. **Ecosystem Development** – Fostering connections across the innovation landscape
4. **Sustainable Growth** – Building a financially viable, member-owned institution
