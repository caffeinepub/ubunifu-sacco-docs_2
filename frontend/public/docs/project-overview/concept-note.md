# Concept Note

## Project Overview

| **Section** | **Details** |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Project Title** | Establishment and Operationalization of UBUNIFU SACCO to Support Technology Startups and Creative Enterprises in Uganda |
| **Implementing Organization** | UBUNIFU SACCO Ltd. |
| **Contact Person** | Mucunguzi Moses, Programme Manager |
| **Project Location** | National: Across Uganda, with regional chapters in Kampala, Mbarara, Gulu, Mbale |
| **Duration** | 5 years (January 2026 – December 2031) |
| **Total Budget** | UGX 50,000,000,000 |

## Project Rationale

The UBUNIFU SACCO will serve as a specialized cooperative financial 
and support institution for technology startups, digital innovators, 
and creative enterprises. It seeks to fill the gap left by traditional 
SACCOs and commercial banks, which often do not cater to high-risk, 
early-stage creative or tech ventures.

## Project Objectives

| **Objective** | **Description** |
|---------------|------------------|
| **i** | Establish an operational SACCO infrastructure for tech and creative enterprises |
| **ii** | Mobilize capital through member contributions, share issuance, private investors, and grants |
| **iii** | Provide access to affordable credit, working capital, and innovation grants |
| **iv** | Facilitate networking, mentorship, incubation support, and market linkages |
| **v** | Strengthen member capacity in financial literacy and commercialization |

## Key Activities

| **Activity** | **Description** |
|--------------|------------------|
| **i** | Develop SACCO governance, bylaws, board structures, and strategic frameworks |
| **ii** | Build and deploy a SACCO management information system and digital channels |
| **iii** | Capital raising: membership drives, share issuance, partner engagements |
| **iv** | Design tailored financial products suited for creative/tech ventures |
| **v** | Establish regional branches or agent networks in key innovation hubs |
| **vi** | Provide mentorship, business incubation, peer networking, pitch events |
| **vii** | Train members in financial literacy and commercialization strategies |
| **viii** | Monitor, evaluate, and iterate services based on member feedback |

## Expected Outcomes

| **Outcome** | **Target** |
|-------------|------------|
| **i** | At least 50,000 members enrolled |
| **ii** | Disbursement of loans and grants to target startups |
| **iii** | Improved business metrics for members |
| **iv** | Enhanced connectivity across the innovation ecosystem |
| **v** | A financially viable SACCO by year 3 |

## Monitoring & Evaluation

| **M&E Component** | **Approach** |
|-------------------|---------------|
| **Quarterly Reports** | Membership growth, loan uptake, repayment rates, member satisfaction |
| **Site Visits** | Audits of branch/agent operations |
| **Feedback Mechanisms** | Member surveys, focus group discussions |
| **Benchmarking** | Performance against peer SACCOs and industry KPIs |
| **Impact Evaluation** | Midterm and endline assessments |

## Sustainability Plan

| **Strategy** | **Description** |
|--------------|------------------|
| **Shareholding Model** | Members own equity in the SACCO |
| **Revenue Generation** | Interest on loans, service fees, advisory services |
| **Risk Management** | Prudent loan recovery policies and reserves |
| **Partnerships** | Co-funding and guarantee schemes with investors and government |
| **Adaptive Services** | Continuously adapt to member needs |
| **Governance** | Strengthen board accountability and oversight |
