# Literature Review

## Introduction

This section reviews existing literature on cooperative finance, digital financial services, and financial inclusion strategies relevant to UBUNIFU SACCO's project design.

## Key Themes

### 1. Cooperative Finance
Research by ILO (2020) shows that SACCOs outperform commercial banks in member satisfaction and loan accessibility for low-income groups.

### 2. Digital Financial Services
The GSMA Mobile Money Report (2023) documents 1.6 billion registered mobile money accounts globally.

### 3. Financial Inclusion
The World Bank Findex (2021) highlights that women, rural populations, and youth remain the most financially excluded groups.
