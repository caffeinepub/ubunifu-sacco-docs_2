# Problem Statement

## Background

Despite Kenya's progress in mobile money adoption, millions of low-income earners remain excluded from formal savings and credit systems. Key barriers include:

- Lack of collateral for traditional bank loans
- High transaction costs
- Geographic remoteness from financial institutions
- Low financial literacy
- Mistrust of formal financial systems

## Scale of the Problem

| Indicator | National Average | Target Communities |
|---|---|---|
| Financial Inclusion Rate | 83% | 54% |
| Formal Credit Access | 35% | 18% |
| Digital Payment Usage | 79% | 41% |
| Savings Account Ownership | 67% | 30% |

## Gap Analysis

UBUNIFU SACCO identifies a critical gap: **technology-enabled cooperative finance** that combines the trust of community-based organizations with the efficiency of digital platforms.
