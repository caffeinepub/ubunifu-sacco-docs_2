# Staffing Needs

## Staffing Requirements by Department

| **Position / Department** | **No. of Staff** | **Key Qualifications** | **Core Responsibilities** |
|---------------------------|------------------|------------------------|---------------------------|
| **Executive / Leadership Team** | 10 | Bachelor's or higher in Finance/Business Admin; 7–10 years in financial institutions or startup financing | Strategic direction, operations oversight, compliance, external partnerships, fundraising |
| **SACCO Manager / CEO** | 1 | Master's or Bachelor's with strong track record in managing SACCOs or microfinance | Oversee daily operations, ensure financial & social objectives are met, governance liaison |
| **Finance & Accounting** | 8–12 | Degrees in Accounting/Finance; CPA/ACCA; experience in SACCOs or MFIs | Manage accounts, budgeting, financial reporting, auditing, statutory compliance |
| **Credit / Loans Department** | 23–35 | Risk management training; ability to assess startup/creative enterprise risk; alternative credit scoring experience | Loan appraisal, monitoring, recovery, credit risk evaluations, managing delinquency |
| **Member Services / Customer Relations** | 13–24 | Strong customer service; basic knowledge of financial products; experience with digital platforms; multilingual preferred | Member onboarding, support, savings mobilization, promotional campaigns |
| **Technology / IT & Innovation** | 8–13 | Degrees in Computer Science or IT; experience in fintech, MIS, data security, mobile/web platforms | Set up/maintain SACCO MIS, develop mobile/web app, cybersecurity, lead innovation pilots |
| **Business Development / Partnerships** | 5 | Experience in startup ecosystems, incubation, innovation networks; networking and negotiation ability | Identify and manage partnerships, organize incubator programs, support grant access |
| **Operations & Compliance / Risk Management** | 6–13 | Background in internal audit, compliance, risk, legal/regulatory frameworks, AML | Ensure compliance, internal audit, risk assessments, fraud prevention |
| **Communications, Marketing & Outreach** | 5 | Training in communications/marketing; experience in digital marketing; understanding of creative/tech sectors | Branding, marketing strategy, outreach, digital/social media, stakeholder communications |
| **Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL)** | 3–5 | M&E experience in financial or development sector; KPI development, impact measurement, data analysis | Design MEL framework, track performance, gather feedback, periodic reporting |
| **Administrative & Support Staff** | 8–13 | Diploma in business administration; good organizational and record-keeping skills | Front desk, clerical duties, file management, scheduling, logistics |

## Total Staff Complement
**98–146 staff over 5 years (phased hiring)**
