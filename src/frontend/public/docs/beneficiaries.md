# Beneficiaries

## Primary Beneficiaries

UBUNIFU SACCO's project directly targets **5,000 low-income earners** in peri-urban and rural Kenya:

| Category | Target Number | % of Total |
|---|---|---|
| Women | 3,000 | 60% |
| Youth (18-35) | 2,000 | 40% |
| Persons with Disabilities | 250 | 5% |
| Small Business Owners | 1,500 | 30% |
| Informal Sector Workers | 3,500 | 70% |

## Secondary Beneficiaries
- Families of primary beneficiaries (~15,000 individuals)
- Local business ecosystems stimulated by increased credit access
- Community agents who earn commissions (~200 agents)

## Geographic Targeting

| County | Target Members |
|---|---|
| Nairobi | 2,000 |
| Kiambu | 1,000 |
| Machakos | 800 |
| Murang'a | 700 |
| Kajiado | 500 |

## Beneficiary Selection Criteria
1. Kenyan citizen aged 18 years and above
2. Resident in target counties
3. Regular income (formal or informal)
4. Willing to save a minimum of KES 500/month
