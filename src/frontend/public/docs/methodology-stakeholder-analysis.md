# Stakeholder Analysis

## Stakeholder Map

| Stakeholder | Role | Interest | Influence | Engagement Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| SACCO Members | Primary beneficiaries | High | Medium | Consultation, feedback loops |
| Board of Directors | Governance | High | High | Regular reporting |
| SASRA | Regulatory oversight | Medium | High | Compliance reporting |
| Technology Partners | Platform delivery | Medium | Medium | Contractual agreements |
| Community Leaders | Mobilization | High | Medium | Partnerships |
| Grant Donors | Funding | Medium | High | Quarterly reports |
| Staff | Delivery | High | Medium | Training, engagement |

## Risk-Interest Grid

- **Manage Closely**: SASRA, Board, Donors
- **Keep Satisfied**: Technology Partners
- **Keep Informed**: Community Leaders
- **Monitor**: General Public

## Engagement Plan
Regular stakeholder forums will be held quarterly to share progress, gather feedback, and address emerging issues.
