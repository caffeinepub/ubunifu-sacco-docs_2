# Customer Segments

## Segment Overview

UBUNIFU SACCO serves five primary customer segments:

### Segment 1: Urban Informal Workers
- Market traders, hawkers, bodaboda riders
- Need: Emergency loans, flexible savings
- Channel: Mobile app, USSD, agent

### Segment 2: Young Professionals
- Graduates, early-career employees
- Need: Career development loans, investment products
- Channel: Mobile app

### Segment 3: Women Entrepreneurs
- Small and micro business owners
- Need: Business loans, group savings
- Channel: Agent network, mobile app

### Segment 4: Rural Farmers
- Smallholder farmers
- Need: Seasonal loans, savings
- Channel: USSD, agent

### Segment 5: Salaried Employees
- Teachers, nurses, civil servants
- Need: Development and school fees loans
- Channel: Payroll deduction, mobile app
